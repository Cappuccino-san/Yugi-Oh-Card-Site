Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the cards are authentic?

All our cards are verified by certified Yu-Gi-Oh experts. We use advanced authentication methods and provide detailed condition reports with every purchase.

What's your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee.

Can I return cards if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We offer a 30-day return policy. Cards must be returned in the same condition they were received, with original packaging.
